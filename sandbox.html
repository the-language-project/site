Sandbox is an environment cultivated to support mini projects that align with The Language Project's mission of promoting multilingual education nationwide.


In technical fields, the term 'sandbox' refers to an incubator of sorts, a safe place to experiment and make mistakes. Thus, the Sandbox is an initiative that hopes to empower individuals passionate about language to spread this love through personal projects they conduct. There's no doubt that among all the fields out there, technology is growing the quickest. With its fast growth comes an enormous range of applications. Thus, as an inherent part of The Language Project, the Sandbox intends to be at the intersection between language and tech. It is TLP's hope that in the long run, this union will help spread awareness on the power and significance of multilingual education.
