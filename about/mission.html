Our Mission


The Language Project is a nonprofit organization that seeks to promote multilingual education for all. Our major objectives include:

  • Ensuring that everyone has access to the resources and opportunities necessary to learn another language
  • Building a strong alliance of diverse advocates to spread awareness on the importance of language education
  • Seeking out partnerships with organizations who share a similar vision
  • Empowering the next generation of youth passionate about multilingualism to take initiative in their local communities
  • Continuously seek out innovative ways to drive language advocacy forward
