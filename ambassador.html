Establishing a language and technology-enthusiastic youth with aspirations to create an impact on technology and language.

The Language Project has recently introduced a global Youth Ambassador Program for students with the goal of accelerating language activism and representation. This will also emphasize the importance of global involvement in preserving different languages amidst globalization encouraging youth between the ages of 13-18 to be actively involved in such activities.



  • Create posters or blurbs to promote your language
  • Curate short videos/skits to be activists of language representation
  • Provide/collect language learning resources
