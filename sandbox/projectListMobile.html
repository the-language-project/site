Solved Chinese


Tags: Chinese

Solved Chinese was founded about two years ago when the founders saw their classmates in the Chinese program painfully memorizing vocabulary by rote, when the experience could have been easier, more fun, and more culturally meaningful. This inspired them to start this project to employ methods specifically tailored to the Chinese language such as character decomposition, pictographic memory aids, and three-way review questions.


    The team is currently looking for individuals who fit one of the following categories:
  1. familiar with (or want extra practice with) HTML/JS/CSS/React for Front-end, or
  2. familiar with (or want extra practice with) Python/Django for Backend, or
  3. a Chinese-as-a-foreign-language learner
